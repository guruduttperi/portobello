Groceristar

Designing a platform to simplify meal planning, eat healthy and save money.

Overview


During the Summer of 2018, I worked as a UX Design Intern at Groceristar, a startup based out of Ternovka, Ukraine. I collaborated directly with the CEO, Arthur Tkachenko, to redesign their Minimum Viable Product - “Meal Planning UX”. The goal of “Meal Planning UX” is to help people simplify planning meals, and it allows people to browse menus, plan meals and be aware of the calories they consume every day. It also allows people to set diet plans and save money, using machine learning algorithms.

I closely followed their brand guide and designed their web application and cross-platform mobile application from scratch. This was my first design internship and it helped me understand that design is hard and that there are a lot of things to be considered when designing. It isn't just about pretty visuals, but it has to follow a lot of principles such as balance, alignment, and space.
